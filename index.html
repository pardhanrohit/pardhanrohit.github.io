About Us

At Pix‑Nova, we believe in the magic of memory. We're a creative studio specializing in premium photo magnets that don’t just decorate spaces — they tell stories. From candid family moments to milestone celebrations, our mission is to help you capture, cherish, and share your most meaningful memories in beautiful, tangible form.

What started as a passion project quickly evolved into a heartfelt brand that puts storytelling and emotion at the center of every design. We take pride in thoughtful craftsmanship, personal touches, and ensuring each order brings a smile. Whether it’s for your fridge, locker, workspace, or a surprise gift — Pix‑Nova is where your moments stick.
